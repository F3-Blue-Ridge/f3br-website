We are F3 Blue Ridge.

Free and open to all men.

Yes, you read that right - FREE as in no cost. There’s no joining fee, monthly membership or expenses in the fine print. No catch. And we welcome men of all sizes, ages, athletic abilities, colors and creeds. Seriously. You are welcome here.

Get Started
Fitness

Open to all men of any fitness level. Workouts are peer-led and designed to be challenging for all, while not leaving anyone behind.

Fellowship

Bonds of genuine friendship formed in the pre-dawn Gloom and strengthened through social gatherings, races, and endurance challenges​

Faith

We define this as simply a belief in something bigger than yourself. F3 is open to men of all faiths and no faith.

Our Core Principles

Free of Charge
Open to all men
Held outdoors, rain or shine, heat or cold
Peer-led in a rotating fashion
